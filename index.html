🔐 Cyber Hygiene Self-Assessment

Answer honestly. This takes less than 2 minutes.

1️⃣ Do you use a different password for your email than for other accounts?
2️⃣ Are your passwords at least 12 characters long?
3️⃣ Do you use multi-factor authentication (MFA) on important accounts?
4️⃣ Do you use a password manager?
5️⃣ Do you install updates when available?
6️⃣ Do you verify unexpected emails or texts before clicking links?
7️⃣ Do you avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive logins (or use a VPN)?
8️⃣ Do you lock your devices with PIN, fingerprint, or face ID?
9️⃣ Do you back up important files?
🔟 If you clicked a suspicious link, would you know what to do next?
